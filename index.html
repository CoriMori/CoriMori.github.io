About Me

My name is Cori Mori. I recently graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) majoring in Game Design and Development and minoring in Psychology. My passion for level design and environment building comes from a childhood spent playing games and imagining worlds of my own. While I would prefer to design, I am fully capable of developing game and web applications. In short, I like to consider myself a dev-capable designer.

Schmack

Citizen 815 is a 2D platformer focusing on fluid movement mechanics, exploration and narrative. In Spring 2020, my partner Nick Rassmussen and I began working on this full time in the MAGIC Maker Program after receiving a grant from MAGIC Spell Studios. I acted as the designer and spent most of the semester fleshing out the design for our demo and planning ahead for our continued development.

Ice Level-BattleBlock Theater

This is a simple level for BattleBlock Theater made using their in game level editor. My goal with this level was to make it fast paced, somewhat difficult, but still well balanced. This level went through a few different iterations, mainly on the section with the laser, due to it being too difficult. However, I feel that I have achieved my initial goal without sacrificing too much difficulty. Click here to view a playthrough of the level.

Base Escape-Half-Life 2

This is a simple level for Half-Life 2 made using the Hammer Editor. This level implies some sort of escape narrative and features a vignette, both which add to the depth and make the level feel more immersive and developed. This level went through several playtests and iterations which encouraged me to expand the level and balance it so it was a bit more challenging. Click here to view a playthrough of the level.

Material Madness

Material Madness is a mobile platformer where you play as a rock with the ability to transform into different objects to traverse the level. For this project I worked as the level designer. I designed 3 levels that went through various iterations due to feedback from playtests. I was also responsible for building the environment with an asset pack.

Quiet Thoughts

Quiet Thoughts is a 2D platformer with a difficulty levle similar to Cuphead. I worked as the level designer for this game. I designed all the platforming areas and assisted in playtesting. Quiet Thoughts is set to release a demo in Febuary 2021. Check out the game here.
