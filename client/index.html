About Me

I graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology in 2021 with a degree in Game Design and Development and a minor in psychology. For the past few years I have worked as a developer at a small studio where I was often the only developer on the project. This experience has allowed me to learn how to work quickly and efficently while under pressure due to tight timelines. When I wasn't working on projects, I was working on improving the engine, workflow, and various Research and Development Projects.

In my free time, I work on a variety of small projects to keep my other skills sharp. I primarily develop these projects in Unity using C#, but occasionally dabel in Unreal and C++. One of the main things I tend to focus on in these projects is level design. When I was a kid, I spent a lot of time making paper prototypes of levels and that passion for level design never went away. So while I have spent the past few year working as a full time developer, I never let my design skills get rusty.

Quiet Thoughts

Quiet Thoughts is a 2D platformer game designed to be very challenging to players. As the level designer for this project, I was tasked with designing all the platforming areas. In each area I created multiple pathways through for players to take and chose spawn locations for various enemies and obstacles. I also assisted in playtesting the game prior to release.
